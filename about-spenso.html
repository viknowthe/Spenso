About Spenso

Spenso is a modern personal finance app designed to help you track expenses, split bills, and stay in control of your money — without complexity.

```

Why Spenso?

Managing money shouldn't feel like a chore. Spenso focuses on simplicity, speed, and clarity so you can focus on what matters — not spreadsheets.

Key Features

  • 📊 Easy expense tracking
  • 👥 Split bills with friends and groups
  • ⚖️ Smart balance calculations
  • 📝 Quick notes and reminders
  • ⚡ Fast, minimal, and distraction-free UI

Privacy First

Your data belongs to you. Spenso is built with a privacy-first approach, ensuring your financial information stays secure and under your control.

Our Vision

We aim to make personal finance simple, intuitive, and accessible for everyone — whether you're tracking daily expenses or managing shared costs.
```
