Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe in Spenso?

Yes. Your data is stored locally on your device. Spenso does not collect, sell, or share your personal financial data. Your information remains under your control.

How can I secure my data?

You can enable App Lock or biometric authentication (such as fingerprint) from the Settings screen to protect your notes and financial data.

What happens if I forget my app lock PIN?

For security reasons, locked data cannot be recovered if access credentials (PIN or biometric authentication) are lost. Please keep them सुरक्षित.

Are my notes and expenses backed up?

Currently, Spenso stores data only on your device. Cloud backup and restore features may be introduced in future updates.

How does expense tracking work?

You can add and manage your daily expenses from the Money section. Spenso helps you organize spending clearly so you always know where your money goes.

How does bill splitting work?

Add participants and expenses in a group, and Spenso will automatically calculate how much each person owes or is owed — simplifying shared expenses.

Can I recover deleted data?

No. Deleted notes or expenses cannot be restored. Please review carefully before deleting any data.
